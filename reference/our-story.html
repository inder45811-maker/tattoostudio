Making my mark.

How Making Marks began — and the name it carries.

The name

In memory of Mark

Making Marks Tattoo Co. was founded in 2021 in memory of my dad, Mark. He wasn’t just my biggest supporter in tattooing — he was my best mate. After losing him suddenly in 2019, it felt right to carry his legacy on.

Now it’s time to make my mark.

— Connor Humpage, founder

The studio

Set in the heart of Royal Leamington Spa on Regent Street, the studio was built to turn getting tattooed into something you look forward to — a calm, considered space with interiors to match the work that comes out of it.

How we work

We specialise in custom, high-quality black & grey tattoos. Every design is created around you: bring us a clear idea, a few references, or just a feeling, and we’ll design a piece that’s yours alone. Whether it’s a full sleeve in fine realism or a single line of script, the brief always starts with you.

Ready to start? Tattoo enquiries are taken through Instagram DM — drop us a message with your idea and we’ll take it from there.
